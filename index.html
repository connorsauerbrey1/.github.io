Most businesses are leaking time and leads because their tools don't talk to each other. I build the connective tissue — so from first touch to closed deal, nothing falls through the cracks.

CRM Implementation

Full backend setup and configuration of CRM platforms — pipelines, automations, tagging logic, custom fields, and reporting dashboards built for your actual workflow, not a generic template.

About Me

A builder with a marketer's brain

I've spent my entire adult life working in marketing and leasing — and what I found was that the difference between a business that grows and one that stalls isn't effort. It's systems.

That realization turned into an obsession with how workflows are built. I've spent years learning how to automate the friction out of operations while keeping the human moments that actually convert leads — because nobody closes a deal with a robot.

Today I work with businesses to build the backend infrastructure that lets their teams do less busywork and more of what they're actually good at. The tech is invisible when it's done right.
